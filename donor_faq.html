Help & FAQ

Common questions about donating.

Generally, donors must be at least 18 years old, weigh at least 45kg, and be in good health. Specific eligibility depends on your health history screening.

You may feel a slight pinch when the needle goes in, but the process is usually painless. Most donors find it very manageable.

The actual donation takes only 10-15 minutes. However, the entire process (registration, screening, and recovery) takes about 45 minutes to an hour.

You generally need to wait 56 days (8 weeks) between whole blood donations to allow your body to replenish its red blood cells.
